Privacy

Privacy Policy

This statement explains how Maza Studios collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards personal information for all visitors, clients, and partners.

Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly, including names, contact details, project briefs, and payment details when relevant. We may also capture technical data such as IP address, device identifiers, cookies, and analytics to maintain platform performance.

How We Use Information

Data is processed to deliver services, personalize experiences, provide support, issue invoices, and improve our products. We may send service announcements or marketing updates, and you may opt out at any time.

Sharing & Disclosure

We share data only with trusted vendors bound by confidentiality agreements, to comply with legal obligations, or during corporate transactions. We do not sell personal data.

Security

We implement encryption, access controls, and monitoring to protect information. While no system is entirely infallible, we continually review safeguards to reduce risk.

Your Rights

You may request access, corrections, deletion, or portability of your personal data. Contact us at privacy@mazastudios.com to exercise these rights.

International Transfers

When transferring data across regions we rely on approved safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses or equivalent frameworks.

Cookies

We use functional and analytical cookies to maintain sessions and measure engagement. You may adjust browser settings to limit cookies; however, some features may not function properly.

Updates

We will update this policy as practices evolve. Significant changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

Contact

You can reach the privacy team at privacy@mazastudios.com or via our contact form. Postal inquiries can be mailed to 27 Division St, New York, NY 10002, USA.
